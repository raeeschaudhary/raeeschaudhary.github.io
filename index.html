About

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences, at Rochester Institute of Technology (RTI) and a Graduate Research Assistant at Sappi-RIT Digital Innovation Lab. at RIT.

I am researching and investigating methods and artifacts that facilitate building trust between humans and computers through interactivity. I am currently working on evaluating novel interactive applications in collaboration with Digital Transformation Initiative at Sappi under the supervision of Dr Konstantinos Papangelis who is also a lab director at Niantic x RIT Geo Games and Media Research Lab and Dr Vassilis-Javed Khan. who is UX scientist at Sappi Europe, Belgium.

I obtained a master's degree in software engineering from Bahria University Islamabad with a gold medal and an award for academic excellence. I have experience in custom front-end and back-end software development in my initial career. I also worked in academics at Mirpur University of Science and Technology performing teaching and research activities. I have published my work in various reputed conferences and journals.
