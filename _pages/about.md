I am a Graduate Research Assistant and a PhD Candidate in <a href="https://www.rit.edu/computing/sappi" target="_blank">Sappi-RIT Digtal Transformation Lab.</a> at Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences, <a href="https://www.rit.edu" target="_blank">Rochester Institute of Technology</a> (RTI), Rochester, New York, USA. 

I am interested to investigate building trust between human and computer through interactivity. I am currently working in realizing and evaluating novel interactive applications in collaboration with Digital Transformation Initiative at <a href="https://www.sappi.com">Sappi</a> under the supervision of Dr <a href="https://www.rit.edu/directory/kxpigm-konstantinos-papangelis" target="_blank">Konstantinos Papangelis</a> and Dr <a href="http://khan.gr/" target="_blank">Vassilis-Javed Khan.</a> 

I obtained a masters degree in software engineering from the <a href="https://www.bahria.edu.pk/buic" target="_blank">Bahria University Islamabad</a> with gold medal and an award for academic excellence. I have worked in <a href="https://must.edu.pk/" target="_blank"> Mirpur Uninversity of Science and Technology  where I participated in teaching and research activities since the year 2014. I have several publications in various reputed conferences and journals.
